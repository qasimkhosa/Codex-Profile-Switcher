# Privacy Policy

Codex Profile Switcher is designed as a local Windows utility.

## Local Data

The app stores Codex profile data locally on your Windows machine so it can switch between profiles. This local data can include Codex login state and should be treated as sensitive.

## Data Sent For Trial And Licensing

For trial and licensing checks, the app may send:

- A hardware-derived identifier.
- App version.
- Product ID.
- License key entered by the user.

This is used to validate trial status and Polar license status.

## Data Not Requested

The app does not ask for:

- Your ChatGPT or OpenAI password.
- Your OpenAI API key.
- Your browser cookies.

## Third Parties

- Polar is used for checkout and license validation.
- Supabase is used for server-side trial validation.
